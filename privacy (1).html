Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025  ·  Effective immediately

This Privacy Policy describes how Suntrenia and its products including ContentGenius collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services at suntrenia.com.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when registering an account, including your name and email address. We also collect information about connected social media accounts such as YouTube and TikTok, including account identifiers and channel information, solely to provide our publishing services.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to provide and improve our services, process transactions, send activity notifications, and respond to support requests. We do not use your information for advertising purposes.

TikTok Integration

When you connect your TikTok account, we request only the permissions necessary to publish videos on your behalf using TikTok's official Content Posting API and Login Kit. We never store your TikTok password. You can revoke our access at any time through your TikTok account settings at tiktok.com/settings.

YouTube Integration

When you connect your YouTube account, we use Google's OAuth 2.0 to request permission to upload videos to your channel. We store only the OAuth tokens required to perform uploads. You can revoke access at any time through your Google account settings.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with service providers who assist in operating our platform, subject to strict confidentiality agreements.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at [email protected].
